Incentivizer Views Specification

Elliot Fiske, Thomas Steinke, and Enoch Tsui

Store view

The store view allows students to purchase grade or class-related bonuses with credits they earned from completed challenges.

The store view simply lists all the available Items to purchase for the course specified in the URL. A student can click on the purchase link for a shop item to spend credits and purchase it.

If the AU is the course owner or admin, there will also be a "+" button to create a new shop item.

Attempt view

Students can now click on challenges on their profile to attempt to complete them.

When a student clicks on a challenge that they have not completed, they will be brought to a page where they will be shown a problem and can respond - through a multiple choice or other form entry depending on the form of the challenge.

Additionally, the student profile will include a link to the Store Page for each course they are enrolled in.

Course and Challenge Creation

We will create a simple interface for teachers to create new courses and related challenges.
